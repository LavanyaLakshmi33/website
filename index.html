SEVENTEEN

Introduction

Seventeen, a south korean K-pop group formed by Pledis Entertainment, is a powerhouse of thirteen talented members known for their exceptional music, captivating performances, and impressive self-production skills. Debuted in 2015, the group's diverse range of talents, including singing, rapping, and choreography, has garnered them a strong global following. Their innovative approach to music-making, along with their infectious energy, has solidified their place as one of the leading acts in the K-pop industry.
